Check These Out

 

Dodge

Most difficult game on google playstore....control your character using joystick and dodge the red balls and collect the red balls

 

Avoid

Simple and fun to play....just swipe and move your character and try to avoid the red balls and collect the blue balls

 

Walls

Easy game.....tap on the upper side of your screen to move the red wall up and tap lower side of your screen to move the red wall down....try and match the colors of the balls with the walls

 

Fast Cars

Interesting game with 2 cars and 3 lanes....just tap on the right side of your screen to change the lane of right car and tap on the left side of your screen to change the lane of left car ......collect the blue blocks and dodge the red blocks
